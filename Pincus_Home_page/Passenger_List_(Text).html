Here is the passenger list in a form that is somewhat easier to read. Some interesting things to note:

    • In column 3, the ages of three of the voyagers was changed. The first number is the

        number was typed and then replaced by the second, hand written number.

    • In column 11, the name and address of Samuel’s father. The is the only known

        reference to anyone in Samuel’s family.

    • In column 18, the name and address of Marie’s brother Harris in New York
