Pincus Family Website

 
 
 
 
 

This site documents the family history of Samuel and Maria Pinkusewitsch who are shown in this photo with six of their seven children. Their seventh child, Esther, was not born yet.


There are two pervasive problems when documenting families of this period. Names changed as borders were crossed and birth dates changed depending on the bureaucracy that was asking. In this history we will use the names that we knew them by. Other names they have been know by are listed here. More information on birth dates can be found here.
