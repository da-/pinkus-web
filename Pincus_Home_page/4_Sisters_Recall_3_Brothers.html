During this clip, the four sisters get into a heated discussion about (among other things) Oscar’s age when he came to the US. What we do know from the passenger list of Oscar’s ship (SS Trave arrived in NY 9/15/1907) is that Oscar stated that he was 17 years old.


We also know that on his original application for Social Security (filed on Dec. 5, 1935), Oscar gave his birth date as August 23, 1891. This would have made him 16 years and 1 month when his ship arrived.
