This the the Declaration of Intention that Samuel filled out in 1920. It states the intention, for himself and his wife Mary, to “renounce forever all allegiance and fidelity to any foreign prince, potentate, state, or sovereignty …” In it he also swears “I am not an anarchist; I am not a polygamist nor a believer in the practice of polygamy …”.
