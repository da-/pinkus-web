After declaring their intention in 1920 to become citizens, Samuel and Mary apply for citizenship in 1924. This petition is interesting since it lists their birth dates and the birth dates of their seven children. It appears that all these dates were made up on the spot since they do not correspond to any dates on any other documentation.


Also notice that their son Oscar (whose occupation is listed as a cutter) and his wife Anna acted as witnesses.


By the way, this petition was denied as you can see from the back of this document
